== Installation ==

1. Connect to your Wordpress Admin Panel
1. Click the "Plugins" menu on the left and choose "Add New".
1. NOTE: if you don't have an "Add New" option you can manually add the plugin to your code directory (see instructions below)
1. Search for "Wizpert".
1. Install "Wizpert Button to Share Your Expertise".
1. After installation has finished, click "Activate plugin". 
1. Click the "Settings" menu on the left and choose "Wizpert".
1. Enter your e-mail address.
1. If you don't have a profile yet, follow the instructions sent to you by email to complete your free profile.
1. You can customize the placement of your button on the "Wizpert Settings" page.
 
= How to manually add the Wizpert Button Plugin =
 
1. Click on the red "Download Version x.x" button on the right side of this page.
1. After the download has finished, extract the files.
1. Upload the "wizpert-button" folder to your server in "/wp-content/plugins" directory.
1. Back on the admin panel click the "Plugins" menu on the left. 
1. You should see the Wizpert Button plugin listed in the available plugins. Click the "Activate" link below the plugin name.
1. Click the "Settings" menu on the left and choose "Wizpert".
1. Enter your e-mail address.
1. If you don't have a profile yet, follow the instructions sent to you by email to complete your free profile.
1. You can customize the placement of your button on the "Wizpert Settings" page.


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Do I need a Wizpert profile to use this button? =

Yes. Without it, your visitors will not be able to call you through the button.

= Are Wizpert accounts completely free? =

Yes. There is no charge to create (or use) your Wizpert profile.

= How do I create a Wizpert profile? =

Simply provide your email on the Settings page of the plugin, and follow the instructions to complete your profile.

= What if I am already registered on Wizpert? =

Simply provide your email on the Settings page of the plugin, and the button will point to your existing profile.

= When can I start charging users for my time? =

We require our wizperts to first get sufficient user ratings before charging a fee. If you would like to start charging immediately, please contact us at <a href='mailto:info@wizpert.com?subject=wordpress'>info@wizpert.com</a>.

Check out our <a href='http://wizpert.com/faqs?beta_key=3ba42' target='_blank'>FAQs</a> on the Wizpert site, or contact us at <a href='mailto:info@wizpert.com?subject=wordpress'>info@wizpert.com</a>.
